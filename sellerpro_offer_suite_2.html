The core idea This offer exists for one specific situation: a brand that got on Amazon, did the work, but hit a ceiling and can't break through. More PPC spend doesn't fix a conversion problem — it just accelerates the bleed. Stalled brands need a diagnosis first, a fix second, and external demand third. That's the sequence this system follows. If you haven't launched on Amazon yet, this isn't your offer.
How it works
1
Diagnose before spending
Week 1-2
Full listing audit — copy, images, pricing, conversion rate, PPC account structure, review quality and velocity. Identify whether the problem is the listing, the traffic, or the product-market fit. Fix what's broken before adding external traffic or you're sending people to a leaky bucket.
2
Fix the foundation
Weeks 2-4
Rewrite listing copy, refresh creative, restructure PPC to stop bleeding, accelerate review velocity. Introduce a temporary promotion or coupon if needed to stimulate conversion and send algorithm a signal that the listing deserves attention.
3
Create external demand
Weeks 4-12
UGC sourcing and testing on TikTok and Meta. Traffic driven to Amazon listing creates external ranking signals that PPC cannot replicate. Platform selection and creative strategy tailored to the product category — not every product belongs on TikTok first.

Offer 03
Fan to Content System
For brands with an existing audience or community that isn't being activated for content or Amazon growth.
Investment
$1,500 - $3,500
blueprint or managed retainer
Best for
Local or regional following Farmers market / fair brands Cult product with word-of-mouth Bootstrap budget Wants organic content without paying creator rates
The core idea The most authentic UGC doesn't come from paid creators. It comes from people who already love the product. Brands with a real local following are sitting on a content army they're not using. This system turns existing fans into a structured, gamified content engine that feeds both social growth and Amazon ranking signals, at a fraction of paid UGC cost.
How it works
1
Audience audit + activation blueprint
Week 1-2
Map the existing audience — where are they, how engaged are they, what platforms do they use. Build the full fan activation blueprint: ambassador program structure, QR code strategy for physical touchpoints (fairs, markets, stores), content brief templates, gamification mechanics, and incentive framework.
2
Launch the ambassador engine
Weeks 2-6
Deploy QR codes and ambassador recruitment at physical touchpoints. Launch the incentive structure — product for content, discount codes, insider club access, leaderboard if volume warrants it. First wave of content starts flowing within 2-4 weeks of activation.
3
Content coordination + Amazon amplification
Weeks 6-12+
Coordinate incoming content, select best performers, brief next wave of creators, and direct traffic to Amazon listing. Fan content feeds Amazon's external traffic signal the same way paid UGC does, at dramatically lower cost. Layer in light paid amplification behind winning organic content.

The bigger picture

How the three offers connect into a growth journey

These aren't three versions of the same thing. Each one is built for a specific situation. A client reads their offer and it should feel like it was written entirely for them — because it was.

Entry point A
New to Amazon, no audience
Offer 01 only. Full launch through growth, all in one system. No other offer needed.
Entry point B
New to Amazon, has a following
Offer 01 plus Offer 03. Launch correctly and activate existing fans at the same time. Best ROI on a bootstrap budget.
Entry point C
Already on Amazon, stalled
Offer 02 only. Diagnosis first, fix second, growth third. Offer 01 does not apply — they already launched.

The common thread across all three: Amazon is the engine, but external demand is the fuel. Brands that only run Amazon PPC are idling in a parking lot. Brands that connect off-platform demand to on-platform conversion are the ones that build real organic ranking and real long term margin.

That connection is what SellerPro sells. Everything else is execution.
